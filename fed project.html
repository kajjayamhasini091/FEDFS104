The Preamble

We, the people of India, having solemnly resolved to constitute India into a Sovereign Socialist Secular Democratic Republic and to secure to all its citizens: Justice, Liberty, Equality, and Fraternity...

Fundamental Rights & Duties

Right to Equality

Ensures equal treatment before law for all citizens.

Right to Freedom

Grants freedom of speech, expression, and movement.

Right Against Exploitation

Prohibits forced labor and child labor.

Right to Constitutional Remedies

Allows citizens to approach courts for enforcement of rights.

Fundamental Duties

Encourage respect for national symbols, and promote harmony and environmental protection.
